Helping the Humanverse

The current effect of Ethereum on Earth's environment weighs heavily on NFTECHLORD's digital soul. While his ethereal existence is, like, sooo rewarding for us creatively, it's impossible to ignore the physical realities of his metaphysical ways.

To help offset NFTECHLORD's impact on the planet, 5% of the funds from every NFT sold will be donated to Open Earth, and a further 5% donated to Artnmoe's Green NFT grant.
